NIRHAAR, OUR OWN MAGAZINE,

While we glide through the initial page of a book it presents us with a prologue from anything to everything. In contrast, our magazine goes into the depth of our institute from when the buds began to sprout to the righteous institute it is today. A walk through the memory lane where moments of happiness sparkled in the eyes of our peers for their achievements and the intricate details regarding the construction of the events are crafted into beautiful stories without missing a beat.

Nirhaar 2.0 | 2021

    The magnum opus of 2021, introduces us to the mesmerizing past and captivating present of our esteemed institute, IIIK holds. Nirhaar 2.0 is a emboidment of our dynamic campus life, detailing every cultural festivity, technical symposium, and groundbreaking hackathon. Each page brims with stories of innovation and celebration, capturing the essence of our community's relentless pursuit of knowledge and creativity.The visual brings the word to life as we are moving through the foresaid magical writings throughout the volume.
