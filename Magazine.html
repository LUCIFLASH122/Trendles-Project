OUR OWN MAGAZINE,

While the initial pages of a book often serve as a mere prologue, our magazine dives deep into the rich history of our institute, tracing its journey from humble beginnings to its present stature. It offers a walk down memory lane, where moments of joy and triumph come alive, celebrating the achievements of our peers and capturing the essence of every event in beautifully crafted stories. Complementing the magazine is our photobook, a collection of photographs that serve as a visual anthology. Each image, much like a quote within the pages of a book, tells a unique story. Together, they preserve the vibrant legacy of our community, showcasing the journey and memories that define us.

October Photobook

    "Octo ‘the’ ber's bright blue weather, and the golden leaves falling like confetti, remind us that even as seasons change, the beauty of life endures and lens capture them."
