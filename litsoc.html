THE POWER OF LITERATURE,

in the palm of your hands, amazing isn’t it? The Literary Club of IIITK with its storied past is delighted to present to you the wonders of literature, from all around the world. Marvel in the beauty of words as they twist you by the heartstrings and take you to the cusp of greatness.


Literature

Welcome to the Literature Club! Our club is a haven for book lovers and literary enthusiasts of all levels. We explore a wide range of genres, from classic novels to contemporary works, fostering a deep appreciation for the written word. Our meetings feature book discussions to share insights and interpretations, writing workshops to nurture creativity, and author events to gain new perspectives. Join us to connect with fellow literature lovers, share recommendations, and immerse yourself in the world of books. Whether you're here to discover new reads or showcase your writing, our club is the perfect place to celebrate the joy of literature.

Manga

Welcome to the Manga Club! Our club is a haven for manga enthusiasts of all levels. We explore Japanese comics, delving into genres like action, romance, and fantasy. Our meetings feature reading sessions to discuss favorite series, drawing workshops for artists, and cosplay events for character dress-up. Join us to connect with fellow manga lovers, share recommendations, and immerse yourself in manga culture. Whether you want to discover new stories or showcase your artwork, our club is the perfect place to celebrate this captivating art form.

Quizzing & Debating

Welcome to the Quizzing and Debating Club! Our club is a vibrant community for enthusiasts of all levels. We explore a wide range of topics, from current events to historical facts, fostering a love for knowledge and critical thinking. Our meetings feature quiz sessions to test your knowledge and debates to hone your argumentation skills. Join us to connect with fellow quizzing and debating lovers, share insights, and engage in stimulating discussions. Whether you want to improve your knowledge or polish your debating skills, our club is the perfect place to celebrate the art of inquiry and discourse.
